No Brakes!

No Brakes! is a crazy VR biking game, where you steer your motorized lazychair through challanging environments and moving obstacles. The game can be downloaded at Itch.io (also playable as a non-VR version)

Short trailer

A short trailer of the game.

Street

The player starts in a wide street with easy obstacles. After some time street barriers will pop up and the player has to react quickly to dodge them. At the end of the street the player has to drive on the sidewalk because a moving van is blocking the road, this is the first checkpoint.

Construction

The next area is a construction site, where the game gets more difficult. The player will have to avoid moving wrecking balls, trucks and steel beams. The last challange of the game is driving up to a rocket that would bring the player to the next level, which has not been developped yet.
